= Ansible content navigator

:context: intro-ansible-navigator

[role="_abstract"]

As a content developer, you can use {Navigator} to develop Ansible playbooks, collections, and roles that are compatible with the {PlatformName}. You can use {Navigator} on local development machines, {ExecEnvNameStart} or the {PlatformName}, with seamless and predictable results across them all. {Navigator} also produces an artifact file you can use to help you develop your playbooks and troubleshoot problem areas.
